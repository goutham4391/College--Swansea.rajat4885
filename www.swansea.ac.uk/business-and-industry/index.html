We can work with your organisation to help you overcome your challenges and support your growth

In support of business and industry

Swansea University was founded on the principles of innovation and collaboration. By working with us your organisation can access world-leading research & development expertise, talented graduates & students, professional training and skills development, first class facilities & equipment, and a number of other commercial opportunities.

Due to the ongoing uncertainty and disruption following the outbreak of Covid-19 our business engagement team have fully transitioned to remote working. The team remain on hand to answer your enquiries and discuss how we can collaborate with your organisation during these uncertain times.

Whilst physical events remain on hold our Swansea University LINC business network is operating in a virtual capacity. You can engage with us and organisations from across the region on our LinkedIn group where we’ll share details of upcoming webinars along with the latest on COVID-19 funding and support that is available to organisations in Wales.
