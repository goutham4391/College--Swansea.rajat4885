The strength of our research environment, encompassing all aspects of the research lifecycle, was measured in the Research Excellence Framework 2021  in which 91% of our research environment was judged to be 4* World- leading or Internationally excellent,conducive to creating the highest quality research. 10 Units of Assessment achieved 100% 4 *world-leading or 3* internationally excellent for their environment, including Business and Management, Modern Languages and Social Work and Social Policy. Full results for Research Excellence Framework 2021 can be found here

Today’s complex research questions do not respect the artificial boundaries between traditional disciplines. This requires different ways of working which are more imaginative and dynamic than in the past. Our research environment reflects this; we are combining innovation and excellent facilities  to encourage more work across disciplinary boundaries. The Morgan Advanced Studies Institute (MASI) will provide a home for multidisciplinary research to flourish and will enhance our competitiveness for inter-disciplinary funding streams. 

We proudly hold the European Commission HR Excellence in Research Award in recognition of our commitment to support the career development of all Research Staff. The Swansea University Research Staff Working Group oversees the delivery of the Concordat Action Plan, to support the Career Development of Researchers. We celebrate the achievements of our research community with the Research and Innovation Awards and provide exciting and innnovative competitions such as Research as Art to support creativity and help promote our research to new audiences.

College Research Hubs

Our College Research Hubs have a wealth of knowledge and experience of working in collaboration with external stakeholders such as research sponsors and business partners.

The teams understand the purpose and strategic aims of the University and College.   The hubs are physically sited at College level to support local needs.  Further information on the Hubs is available via our staff intranet.

Training for Researchers

The University provides extensive training for our research staff and postgraduate research students and support for visiting and international researchers.

Research staff have a dedicated member of the Development and Training Service who is also a point of contact for one to one support.   All research staff training courses are mapped to the Vitae Researcher Development Framework (RDF). 

Further information on the following training programmes is available via our staff intranet.

  • Training and Development for Research Students and Supervisors
  • PGR Supervisor Toolkit 
  • Research Integrity training programme 
  • ISS Research Support - Upcoming Events
  • Research, Engagement & Innovation Services Seminar Series
  • Swansea University: LINC engagement network

Promoting Research

A range of platforms and support are available to our community to promote research to a range of stakeholders.  Further information is accesible via our staff intranet.

  • Research Information System (RIS) a single, central repository of published and ongoing research at the University
  • Cronfa - the University's open access research repository, populated by RIS
  • Promoting Research in Media - guidance from the press team on working with the media
  • Research Marketing - guidance from the Business and Research Marketing team 
  • ResearchFish - Research Councils (RCUK) system to report research impact and compliance
  • The Conversation - global, independent articles sourced from the academic and research community.   A local editor provides our community with guidance on submitting articles.
